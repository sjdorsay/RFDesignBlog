About Me

As a recent graduate from the University of Waterloo in Ontario, Canada, I have obtained a Bachelor of Applied Science degree in Electrical Engineering. During my initial two years after graduation, I gained experience in the aerospace industry, where I was engaged in the design of various projects including a data gateway, an A429 bus protocol emulator, and a wideband interface for Jetwave 2.0.
